Our Story

Every Fla vor Tells a Story

Rescued Food is a company founded by a group of friends, made up of Victor Mosquera, Dylan Almendra, Ashly Jaén, Paola González, Yulissa Ojo. Created in collaboration with the Supérate foundation by Alberto Motta. Rescued Food emerged in search of a problem that the world is currently suffering from, and we are based on food science, where we choose food waste, a big problem in Panama today. We began to develop this problem, looking for solutions, where our best and most complete was creating an application where we give courses and guides where we give solutions on how to give other uses to food in poor condition, and with a quick expiration date.
